Support

Help with Track Sets

Email support@trackset.net with your device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Testing and app access

Track Sets does not require a login, subscription, purchase, or special access. The app can be tested immediately after installation.

Data control

Track Sets has no account system. Delete exercises in the app, clear app storage in system settings, or uninstall the app to remove local data.

Privacy

Track Sets has no ads, analytics, network features, or server-side user data. Read the Privacy Policy.
