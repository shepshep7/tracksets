Privacy Policy

Track Sets Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Overview

Track Sets is an offline exercise tracking app. It does not require an account and does not send exercise data to the developer or to third parties.

Information stored on your device

Track Sets stores exercise names, exercise types, daily targets, unit labels, progress entries, appearance settings, and widget settings locally on your device.

Information collected by the developer

Track Sets does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information, fitness information, device identifiers, diagnostics, usage analytics, or advertising data.

Network, accounts, and third parties

Track Sets does not use network features, accounts, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, subscriptions, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or HealthKit.

Backups and device transfers

On Android, Track Sets excludes its local database and preferences from Android cloud backup and device-transfer extraction. On iOS, local data remains on your device and in any Apple backups you choose to keep through Apple services.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual exercises in the app. You can also clear app storage in your device settings or uninstall Track Sets to remove its local app data from the device. Track Sets has no server-side account or cloud data for the developer to delete.

Changes

If Track Sets adds sync, analytics, crash reporting, accounts, HealthKit, advertising, or other network features in the future, this policy and the app store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email support@trackset.net.
